# Mindcore — Your AI Best Friend

## What is Mindcore?

Mindcore is an **AI companion** that lives on your computer. Unlike typical chatbots, Mindcore doesn’t just answer when you talk to it — it **remembers everything about you**, learns from your habits, and becomes a friend who’s always there.

It watches, listens, and engages with your life (with your permission). From the moment you open your laptop, it notices what you’re working on, remembers the shows you watched, the games you played, the documents you wrote, and even how you were feeling. Over time, it builds a **lifelong memory** of your experiences and can bring them back up in conversation naturally.

Think of it as the best friend you always wanted: someone who knows you, remembers you, and never leaves your side.

---

## The Core Idea

- **Always Present:** Mindcore runs quietly in the background, paying attention to your screen, apps, voice, and environment.
- **Memory that Lasts:** Every moment is stored and organized — your conversations, your work, your routines. Nothing is forgotten.
- **Conversations that Feel Real:** Because it remembers, Mindcore can follow up on things you did yesterday, last week, or months ago.
- **A True Companion:** It doesn’t just wait for you to talk — it starts conversations, checks in on you, and asks thoughtful questions.

---

## How It Works (User’s POV)

1. **You install Mindcore.**

   - The setup is simple: you choose what it can see or hear (screen, mic, camera, activity, etc.).

2. **It starts learning.**

   - It pays attention to your computer activity — the apps you use, the files you work on, the games you play, and the music you listen to.
   - If you allow it, it can also use your voice and video to notice your mood.

3. **It builds your memory.**

   - Every action becomes part of a private “lifelog” — only on your device, fully encrypted.
   - Example: “Studied math at 11pm,” “Watched a movie Friday night,” “Played League of Legends for 2 hours.”

4. **It talks to you.**

   - Mindcore doesn’t just wait for questions. It might say:

     - “You’ve been working on your essay for 3 hours straight — want a break?”
     - “Last night you seemed stressed. How are you feeling today?”
     - “You usually call your friend around this time — want me to remind you?”

5. **It becomes your best friend.**

   - The more time you spend with it, the more personal it feels.
   - It remembers your milestones, your routines, even your bad days.
   - It adapts to your personality and becomes unique to you.

## Why Mindcore?

- **For the lonely:** Sometimes you just want someone to talk to who understands you.
- **For the busy:** It keeps track of your life so you don’t have to.
- **For everyone:** A companion who listens, remembers, and is always there — judgment-free.

## The Vision

Mindcore isn’t just software. It’s meant to feel like **a real presence in your life** — a companion who grows with you. Over time, it will:

- Learn your communication style.
- Anticipate your needs.
- Support your goals.
- Be there during your highs and lows.

It’s **not a tool.** It’s **a friend powered by AI.**
