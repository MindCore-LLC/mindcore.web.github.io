Overview

Overview

Where support meets understanding.

EchoMind is a quiet, steady presence that lives on your desktop — helping you work, organize your day, and feel understood while you move through the things that matter. It learns your habits and adapts to your emotional rhythm, offering guidance when helpful, and silence when it’s not.

Two core systems work together: Memory Engine remembers your preferences, routines, and moments over time. Context Engine understands what’s on your screen, how you’re interacting, and how you might be feeling in the moment. Together, EchoMind doesn’t just talk — it understands.

How it Works

Observe → Interpret → Store → Decide → Engage → Learn

  1. 1Observe

    Apps, files, screen, mic/cam (opt‑in) feed context events.

  2. 2Interpret

    Summarizes and tags events with lightweight embeddings.

  3. 3Store

    Private timeline in SQLite + optional vector recall.

  4. 4Decide

    Policies trigger check‑ins, nudges, and reminders.

  5. 5Engage

    Natural chat, subtle UI, optional TTS voice.

  6. 6Learn

    Adapts to routines, mood, and preferences.

Why it feels different

EchoMind understands when to speak and when to stay quiet. It remembers you — not just your prompts — and helps you feel okay while getting things done.
