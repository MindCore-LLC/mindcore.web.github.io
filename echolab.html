EchoLab Research

Forging the future of
Human-AI Symbiosis.

We explore the boundaries where biological cognition meets artificial intelligence, creating systems that don't just compute, but understand.

Our Mission

To advance the science of machine intelligence beyond raw processing power, focusing on empathy, context awareness, and ethical alignment. We believe the next great leap in AI is not just being smarter, but being more human-centric.

Research Pillars

Cognitive Modeling

Simulating human thought processes to create AI that reasons in relatable ways. We study memory formation, attention mechanisms, and decision-making heuristics to build more natural interlocutors.

Affective Computing

Developing systems that can recognize, interpret, and simulate human emotions. Our goal is to bridge the emotional gap, allowing AI to respond with appropriate empathy and tone.

HCI & Interfaces

Reimagining how humans interact with digital systems. From brain-computer interfaces (BCI) concepts to fluid, intent-based UI that predicts user needs before they are explicitly stated.

Ethical AI

Ensuring our systems are safe, unbiased, and privacy-preserving by design. We research techniques for algorithmic transparency and user sovereignty over personal data.

Active Projects

Project Mirror

Self-Reflection AI

An AI agent designed to help users understand their own behavioral patterns through Socratic dialogue and data visualization.

Project Pulse

Biometric Integration

Experimental integration of wearable health data (heart rate, HRV) to modulate AI response styles based on user stress levels.

Project Weaver

Knowledge Synthesis

Algorithms for automatically connecting disparate pieces of information across a user's digital footprint to generate novel insights.
