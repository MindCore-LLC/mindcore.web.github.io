The Core Idea

Not a tool. A friend powered by AI.

Always Present

Runs in the background and pays attention to your screen, apps, voice, and environment — only with your permission.

Memory that Lasts

Builds a private lifelog on your device. Moments become memories that are searchable, retrievable, and useful.

Conversations that Feel Real

Because it remembers, it can follow up on things you did days, weeks, or months ago.

A True Companion

It doesn’t just wait for you to talk — it checks in, nudges, and supports your goals.

How it Works

Observe → Interpret → Store → Decide → Engage → Learn

Observe

Trackers for apps, files, screen, mic/cam (opt‑in) feed events into the daemon.

Interpret

Summarizes, tags, and embeds events into lightweight memories.

Store

Private timeline in SQLite + vector index. Local‑first by design.

Decide

Policies trigger check‑ins, nudges, and reminders based on context.

Engage

Chat naturally; TTS can speak; notifications appear when helpful.

Learn

Adapts to your routines, mood, and preferences over time.

Pluggable Skills

Simple skills/plugins react to triggers and act through a local SDK.

Episodic Memory

Moments become searchable memories with summaries, tags, and embeddings.

Orchestrated Conversation

Retrieval‑augmented prompts with a plan → answer loop and safety self‑check.

Privacy, Consent, and Safety

Private by default. You stay in control.

Local‑first

Data stays on your machine. No cloud sync by default.

Granular Consent

Per‑sensor toggles. You choose what Mindcore can see or hear.

Encryption at Rest

Field‑level AES‑GCM with locked/unlocked control. Passphrase never stored.

Transparency

Daily snapshot and a quick “Do not record” toggle to pause capture anytime.
